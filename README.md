# Hawksaw Ridge

**Genre:**  War, Historical Fiction, Drama
**Director:** Mel Gibson
**based on:** The 2004 documentary The Conscientious Objector and the true story of Desmond Doss
**Released Year:** 2016

## Story Summary
Hacksaw Ridge tells the true story of Desmond Doss, a conscientious objector and U.S. Army medic during WWII who refused to carry a weapon on religious grounds
